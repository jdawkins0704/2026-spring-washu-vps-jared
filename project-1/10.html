Who are we?

We are known for our cozy, farm-to-table comfort food, scratch-made pastries, and coffee, serving generations with brunch, breakfast, lunch, and dinner in University City. We focus on fresh, seasonal ingredients from local farms to create comforting dishes like quiche, pot pies, and house-made baked goods, offering a warm, casual experience with both dine-in and takeaway options, plus a market for local goods.

Why Us?

We stand out for our commitment to farm-fresh, seasonal ingredients, creating modern American comfort food in a charming, historic building that feels like a neighborhood escape, combining a casual restaurant, bakery, and market with housemade goods and local provisions for a truly local, community-focused experience.
