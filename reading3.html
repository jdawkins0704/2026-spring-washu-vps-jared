This reading was truly eye-opening. It shifted my perspective on the way that I see and interact with technology. For one, before reading this article, I had a completely different definition of” cyborgs”. I definitely would not have compared wearable technology to cyborgs by any means. It really made me think about how many “cyborg” devices I own and use on a regular basis. This made me wonder about what other smart home devices qualify, such as a Ring doorbell or a Nest thermostat. Furthermore, it was interesting to learn some new terminology that I never heard before. Words such as “somaforming” and “terraforming” were both foreign to me until this reading, but are now words that I will try to be more cognizant of as I continue through this design class and even after.
