What is a human-made creation that brings out the best in you? Why? It could be an object, tool, work of art, process, system, environment… or something else human-made. What positive qualities does this creation call forth in you? What positive state(s) does it awaken in you? How does it empower you to express your value and strengths? Why does it have this effect on you? Rather than focusing on tools that augment your productivity or performance on instrumental tasks, pay attention to how human-made things uplift you and allow you to be the best version of yourself.

A human-made creation that brings out the beset in me is my Outlook calendar. While that might seem like a crazy choice, I can confidently say that my Outlook calendar helps me stay on task and keeps me in check. I first started using my Outlook calendar my first semester of college. Until this point, I kind of just created a mental calendar of all the tasks I needed to complete as well as when I needed to complete them. While this was somewhat effective in high school, as soon as I got to college that quickly changed. I was no longer able to remember everything all at once and had to dedicate more time to other things such as classes and exams. This human creation calls forth my positive qualities of organization and time management. Because of my outlook calendar, I’m rarely ever late to events and am also always generally ready for things weeks in advance. I’d also say that this empowers me to maximize my strengths because I’m the kind of person who easily forgets certain things, but with this creation I don’t really have to worry about that too much. I’m not sure if this tool “augments my productivity or performance on instrumental tasks” seeing as it only keeps tracks of the time I have to do things, but regardless of that, I do know that it allows me to be the very best version of myself.
